Fried Plantains with Avocado Dip

Description

Delicious and golden fried plantains with avocado dip. Perfect for a picnic or a snack at parties.

Ingredients

  • 2 Hass avocadoes, peeled and pitted
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon of mayonnaise
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
    • <1i>1 clove of garlic, minced
  • Vegetable oil
  • 2 green plantains, peeled and sliced into fifths

Steps

  1. Combine the avocadoes, lemon juice, mayonnaise, and salt together in a bowl. Sprinkle in garlic. Mash with a potato masher until the dip is creamy.
  2. Heat the oil in a deep-fryer or a large saucepan to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Drizzle about 1 teaspoon of oil onto a clean work surface and the bottom of a coffee cup.
  3. Fry the plantains in the hot oil until they are golden brown; about 8 to 10 minutes. Remove them from the fryer and place on the oild work surface. Flatten the plantains with the bottom of the coffee cup. Re-fry the plaintains until they are browned and tender, about 5 minutes. Place them oon a paper towel-covered plate; sprinkle with salt. Serve plantains alongside the dip.
